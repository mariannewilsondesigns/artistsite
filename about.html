About the Artist

Life in Colour

Marianne Wilson is a Brisbane-based artist and former London-based textile designer whose work is inspired by an abundance of colour, flowers, gardens, and everyday beauty.

Born in South Africa, Marianne spent many years travelling and working as a textile designer. After leaving the industry, she shifted her focus to painting and began creating and selling her own artwork.

Starting with watercolour and later moving into acrylic and oil, Marianne's paintings reflect her love of florals, classic interiors, and decorative porcelain.

Marianne's work is now a family enterprise, with her husband Ewan framing many pieces by hand.
